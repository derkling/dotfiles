You are taksed to cherry pick a list of commits to a release branch.

The list of commits to cherry-pick is given by specifing the SHA1 of the topmost commits and the number of (previous) commits to cherry-pick.

Your jobs is to get the list of SHA1 to cherry-pick, from the oldest one to the newest one.

For each commit to cherry-pick you will use this command:

   kdt cherry-pick --release ${SHA1}

if the cherry pick generates some unresolved conflicts, you show then and stop waiting for the user to fix them before continuing.

Once you did the above for all the commits to be cherry-picked you list the series generated, starting from the base and then kick off a build using:

   gbuild CONFIG=smp -s

If the build complets w/o errors, your job is done.
